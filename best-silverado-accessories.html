The Silverado Bottom Line

For Silverado owners, start with WeatherTech floor mats and Coverado seat covers — those two upgrades protect the interior for under $400 total. Add a tonneau cover based on your bed length: BAKFlip MX4 for the 5'10" short bed, TruXedo TruXport for the 6'6" standard bed. The Decked storage system is worth the investment if you use your truck for work. For Silverado-specific tonneau cover recommendations, see our Silverado Tonneau Cover Guide.
