Best Truck Phone Mounts of 2026

The mounts that keep your phone visible without launching it across the cab

Truck phone mounts fail in predictable ways: weak suction cups, too much arm movement, bad dash placement, or magnets that quit once the road gets rough. These are the options that hold up better than average.

1. Bulletpoint Mounting Solutions

Best For Truck Owners

Bulletpoint Dash Mount System

★★★★★ 4.8/5
~$90-$160 depending on vehicle and accessories

Bulletpoint earns the top spot because it is purpose-built for truck and off-road interiors. The modular ball-mount setup is stable, flexible, and much less sketchy than cheap vent or suction mounts.

✓ Pros

  • Excellent stability
  • Vehicle-specific mounting options
  • Expandable system

✗ Cons

  • More expensive than generic mounts
  • Best setups require vehicle-specific kits
  • Can become a rabbit hole of accessories

The Bottom Line

Buy Bulletpoint or Offroam if you care about truck-specific fit and real stability. Buy RAM Mounts if your truck sees rough terrain. Buy iOttie or ESR if you just need an inexpensive commuter solution and are honest about that.
