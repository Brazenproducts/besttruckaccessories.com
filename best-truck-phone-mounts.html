Best Truck Phone Mounts — 2026 Rankings

Your phone is your GPS, music player, and trail guide. These mounts keep it locked in place no matter how rough the road gets.

1. RAM X-Grip with Tough-Claw Base

★★★★★ 9.6/10

RAM Mounts are the industry standard for a reason. The X-Grip holds any phone securely with its spring-loaded arms, and the Tough-Claw base clamps to virtually anything — round bars, flat surfaces, rails. The ball-and-socket joint lets you position your phone at any angle. Military and emergency vehicles use these. Your truck should too.

✓ PROS

  • Bomb-proof construction
  • Universal fit — any phone
  • Infinite angle adjustment
  • Clamps to anything
  • Made in USA

✗ CONS

  • No wireless charging
  • Bulkier than magnetic mounts

2. Peak Design Mobile Car Mount

★★★★★ 9.2/10

If you're in the Peak Design ecosystem (and you should be), their car mount is slick. The magnetic + mechanical lock system means your phone clicks in and stays put — no fumbling with arms or clamps. It's low-profile, looks premium, and the one-hand operation is genuinely one-handed. Requires their case or universal adapter.

✓ PROS

  • One-hand click-in operation
  • Magnetic + mechanical lock
  • Sleek, minimal design
  • Multiple mount options (vent, dash, windshield)

✗ CONS

  • Requires Peak Design case/adapter
  • Expensive ecosystem buy-in

3. iOttie Auto Sense 2

★★★★☆ 8.5/10

The Auto Sense 2 uses a proximity sensor to automatically open and close its arms when you bring your phone near. It's like magic the first time, and it includes Qi wireless charging. The suction cup base is strong and the telescoping arm gives good reach. A great all-rounder for daily driving.

✓ PROS

  • Auto-sensing open/close
  • Built-in Qi wireless charging
  • Strong suction cup
  • Telescoping arm

✗ CONS

  • Sensor can be finicky with cases
  • Suction cup needs re-sticking in heat

4. Quad Lock Car/Truck Mount

★★★★☆ 8.3/10

Quad Lock's twist-lock system is popular with motorcyclists for good reason — it's vibration-proof. The same tech works great in trucks, especially if you're on rough roads regularly. The mount is compact and the lock is satisfying. Like Peak Design, you need their case, but the security is unmatched.

✓ PROS

  • Twist-lock — vibration proof
  • Extremely compact
  • Proven on motorcycles (rougher than trucks)

✗ CONS

  • Requires Quad Lock case
  • Limited angle adjustment

5. LISEN MagSafe Car Mount

★★★★☆ 7.6/10

For iPhone users who want dead-simple magnetic mounting, LISEN's MagSafe mount is hard to beat for the price. Strong magnets, clean design, and it works with MagSafe cases. The vent clip is solid and the ball joint gives decent adjustment. Just know that magnetic mounts have limits on rough terrain — a big bump can knock your phone loose.

✓ PROS

  • Simple magnetic attach
  • Budget-friendly
  • Clean, minimal look

✗ CONS

  • iPhone/MagSafe only (or needs metal ring)
  • Can detach on rough roads
  • Vent clips can break over time
