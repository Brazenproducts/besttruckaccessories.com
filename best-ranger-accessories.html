Best Ford Ranger Accessories 2026

Tonneau covers, floor mats, seat covers, bed racks, bumpers, and lighting for 2019-2024 Ranger

The Ford Ranger came back to the US market in 2019 and immediately carved out a loyal following among mid-size truck buyers who wanted something tougher than a Tacoma but didn't need a full-size F-150. The 2019-2024 Ranger has a solid accessory market, and these are the products that earn their spot.

Lighting

Best Ditch Lights

★★★★★ 4.8/5
~$300-$450

Diode Dynamics makes the best plug-and-play ditch light kit for the Ranger. The SS3 Sport pods mount at the base of the A-pillars using vehicle-specific brackets, and the output is impressive for their compact size. Combo beam gives you both throw and spread.
