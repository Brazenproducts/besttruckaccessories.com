Best Truck Floor Mats of 2026

WeatherTech, Husky Liners, and 3D MAXpider compared by containment, fit, and long-term use

Floor mats sound boring until your carpet is permanently stained, your factory mats slide around, or winter slop gets everywhere. The best truck mats are the ones you forget about because they fit right, hold mess where it belongs, and clean up fast.

1. WeatherTech FloorLiner

Best Overall

WeatherTech FloorLiner

★★★★★ 4.7/5
~$160-$240 per row set

WeatherTech remains the safest recommendation because the molded fit is consistently strong and the containment lip works. They are not the cheapest and not always the softest underfoot, but they do the job better than most alternatives.

✓ Pros

  • Excellent fitment
  • Strong containment walls
  • Easy to hose off
  • Huge vehicle coverage

✗ Cons

  • Premium price
  • Can feel rigid
  • Industrial look

2. Husky Liners WeatherBeater

Best Value

Husky Liners WeatherBeater

★★★★★ 4.7/5
~$140-$210 per row set

Husky usually lands just under WeatherTech on prestige and right beside it on usefulness. In some trucks the fit is even better. If you can save money on Husky for your specific vehicle, that is often the smarter buy.

✓ Pros

  • Great value
  • Excellent coverage
  • Slightly more forgiving material

✗ Cons

  • Less premium branding
  • Styling is plain
  • Vehicle-to-vehicle differences matter

3. 3D MAXpider Kagu

Best Premium Cabin Look

3D MAXpider Kagu Floor Mats

★★★★☆ 4.6/5
~$180-$260

3D MAXpider is what you buy when you want fitted protection without the hard plastic feel. The texture looks better in nicer interiors and the layered construction feels more upscale underfoot. It is strongest for owners who care about cabin appearance.

✓ Pros

  • More refined appearance
  • Good grip and comfort
  • Better for premium interiors

✗ Cons

  • Usually pricier
  • Containment not always as aggressive
  • Less work-truck aesthetic
