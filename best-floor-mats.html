Best Truck Floor Mats — 2026 Rankings

Mud, snow, gravel, coffee — your truck floor sees it all. These mats actually contain the chaos.

1. WeatherTech FloorLiner HP

★★★★★ 9.5/10

WeatherTech basically invented the laser-measured floor mat category, and the FloorLiner HP is their best work yet. The high-wall design contains an absurd amount of liquid, the channels direct everything away from your feet, and the fit is so precise it looks OEM. Yes, they're expensive. Yes, they're worth every penny if you live somewhere with real weather.

✓ PROS

  • Laser-measured perfect fit
  • High-wall containment
  • Made in USA
  • Lifetime warranty
  • Easy to remove and hose off

✗ CONS

  • Premium price ($150-200 for front set)
  • Can curl at edges in extreme heat

2. Husky Liners X-Act Contour

★★★★★ 9.3/10

Husky's X-Act Contour is the mat that gives WeatherTech nightmares. Same laser-measured fit, similar high walls, but with a softer rubberized feel that's more comfortable underfoot. They also tend to run $20-30 cheaper. The DirtGuard ridges on the bottom keep the mat from sliding. Honestly, you can't go wrong with either brand.

✓ PROS

  • Precision laser-measured fit
  • Softer, more comfortable material
  • Slightly cheaper than WeatherTech
  • Lifetime warranty

✗ CONS

  • Slightly lower walls than WeatherTech
  • Material can stain from certain boots
Check Price on Amazon →

3. MAXLINER All-Weather Floor Mats

★★★★☆ 8.4/10

MAXLINER delivers about 85% of the WeatherTech experience at 60% of the price. The fit is vehicle-specific (not universal), the walls are high enough to matter, and the material is durable TPE rubber. Where they lose points is in the finer details — edge finishing isn't as clean, and the channels aren't quite as effective at directing water.

✓ PROS

  • Great value for custom fit
  • High-wall design
  • Odorless TPE material

✗ CONS

  • Edge finishing not as refined
  • Thinner material than premium options
Check Price on Amazon →

4. Lasfit All-Weather Floor Mats

★★★★☆ 8.1/10

The newcomer that's been turning heads. Lasfit offers custom-fit TPE mats with impressive wall height and a textured surface that hides dirt well. They're aggressively priced and the fit is genuinely good for most popular trucks. Not quite the track record of WeatherTech or Husky, but the product itself is solid.

✓ PROS

  • Aggressive pricing
  • Custom fit for popular models
  • Good wall height

✗ CONS

  • Newer brand — less proven
  • Limited vehicle coverage
Check Price on Amazon →

5. Motor Trend FlexTough Plus

★★★☆☆ 7.0/10

The budget option. These are universal-fit rubber mats that you trim to size. They won't hug your floor like custom mats, but they'll keep mud and water off your carpet for under $40. If you're leasing or just need something temporary, they work. For a truck you plan to keep? Spend the extra on custom fit.

✓ PROS

  • Under $40
  • Trimmable to fit
  • Heavy-duty rubber

✗ CONS

  • Universal fit — gaps and bunching
  • Can slide without retention hooks
  • Looks cheap
