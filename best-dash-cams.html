Best Truck Dash Cams of 2026

The cameras worth trusting when your truck gets blamed for something it did not do

A dash cam is one of the least exciting accessories to buy and one of the most useful once something happens. For trucks, wide-angle coverage, heat tolerance, and reliable parking mode matter more than gimmicks.

1. VIOFO A229 Pro

Best Overall

VIOFO A229 Pro Front + Rear

★★★★★ 4.7/5
~$280-$360

The VIOFO A229 Pro is the best current all-around buy if you want image quality, good app support, and real two-channel coverage. It is not the cheapest, but it is one of the easiest recommendations to defend.

✓ Pros

  • Excellent image quality
  • Strong front and rear package
  • Good enthusiast reputation

✗ Cons

  • Not the cheapest option
  • Hardwire kit adds cost
  • Setup takes some effort

The Bottom Line

The VIOFO A229 Pro is the most complete recommendation. REDTIGER is the value play. Garmin is the clean install choice. If you actually care about parked monitoring on an expensive truck, Thinkware earns its price more than most premium electronics do.
